BAT-R-UP

The faces behind the company.

McKay Barlow:

McKay is the owner of Camarillo Bat-R-Up. He has lived in Camarillo for almost his entire life and is very familiar with the area and the people. He loves baseball, and has two sons currently playing making hi very actively engaged in the sport. He has coached all of his children's teams so he know many of the youth in the area.

Jeff Tacket:

Jeff Tacket is a former MLB player for the Baltimore Orioles. He now offers private lessons at Bat-R-Up! He has worked with the youth of Camarillo for many years, and enjoys helping them improve their skills.
